## Project Title

Side Quest Week 3: Pink World - A small act of kindness

---

## Description

This project is a short interactive story created using p5.js and organized across multiple game states and files. The player explores a pink-themed world and is presented with a simple choice about whether to help someone or walk past them. The story branches based on this decision and ends with a different outcome, focusing on how small acts of kindness can affect an experience.

---

## Interaction Instructions

- Click Start to begin the story.
- Read the text shown on each screen.
- Click one of the on-screen buttons to make a choice.
- Continue clicking through the story until you reach an ending.
- Click Play Again to restart the experience.
  All interaction is done using mouse clicks.
